# Dispatcher Quick Start Guide

**New Streamlined Dispatch Workflow** - Get jobs out the door in 5 clicks!

---

## 🚀 Quick Dispatch (Recommended)

**Use this for daily dispatch operations**

1. **Click**: "Dispatch Workflow" in sidebar (⭐ icon)

2. **Step 1 - Select Jobs**:
   - Check boxes next to pending jobs
   - Or click "Select All" to choose all pending jobs
   - Click "Next"

3. **Step 2 - Select Vehicles**:
   - Check boxes for available vehicles
   - Or click "Select All"
   - Click "Next"

4. **Step 3 - Optimize**:
   - Review job/vehicle count
   - Click "Generate Optimized Routes"
   - System auto-distributes jobs across vehicles
   - Auto-advances to Step 4

5. **Step 4 - Assign Drivers**:
   - Select driver from dropdown for each route
   - View route details (stops, distance, vehicle)
   - Click "Next"

6. **Step 5 - Dispatch**:
   - Review all routes
   - Click "Dispatch X Route(s)"
   - Done! Workflow auto-resets

**Time**: ~30 seconds for 5-10 jobs

## 📋 Jobs Management

**Use this to manage job lifecycle**

### Creating Jobs:
1. Click "Jobs" in sidebar
2. Click "Create Job" button
3. Choose "Existing Customer" or "New Customer" tab
4. Fill in delivery address
5. Select pickup address mode:
   - "Use Last Stop" (default) - picks up from previous delivery
   - "Custom Address" - specific pickup location
6. Set priority (low/normal/high/urgent)
7. Click "Create"

### Viewing Jobs:
- **Active Jobs Tab**: Pending and in-progress jobs
- **Completed Jobs Tab**: Recently finished jobs
- **Archived Tab**: Historical jobs (retrievable)

### Archiving:
- **Auto-Archive**: Completed jobs auto-archive after 5 seconds
- **Manual Archive**: Click "Archive Completed (X)" button to bulk archive
- **Individual Archive**: Click archive icon on any completed job
- **Unarchive**: Go to Archived tab, click unarchive icon

**Benefits**: Clean active view, historical data preserved

## 🎯 Best Practices

### Morning Dispatch:
1. Review Active Jobs tab
2. Archive yesterday's completed jobs
3. Use Dispatch Workflow for today's jobs
4. Assign drivers based on availability

### Mid-Day:
1. Check Active Jobs for new urgent jobs
2. Create new jobs as they come in
3. Manually assign to existing routes if needed

### End of Day:
1. Archive all completed jobs
2. Review Archived tab for reporting
3. Check Active Jobs for any unfinished work

## ❓ Troubleshooting

**"No available vehicles"**:
- Go to Vehicles page
- Change vehicle status to "AVAILABLE"

**"No active drivers"**:
- Go to Drivers page
- Ensure driver status is "ACTIVE"

**"Jobs not showing"**:
- Click "Refresh" button
- Check job status filter

**"Archive button disabled"**:
- No completed jobs to archive
- Wait for jobs to complete
